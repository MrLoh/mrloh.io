Hi, my name is **Tobias Lohse**. I’m a generalist software engineer who enjoys turning emerging AI capabilities into production systems: pragmatic architecture, thoughtful product UX, and nimble teams that can keep improving after launch.

Over the past decade I’ve worked as a co-founder, founding engineer, Head of Engineering, and engineering manager/director. The domains have varied — AI infrastructure, health-tech, climate, cinema — but the work often comes back to the same themes: making ambiguous ideas concrete, staying hands-on across research, product, and platform engineering, and helping small teams move with clarity.

On the technical side, I like the connective tissue: model and agent behavior, product constraints, data flows, APIs, deployment, and the tests and traces that make all of it operable. I tend to reach for TypeScript, Python, React, PyTorch, Hugging Face, Postgres, Docker, and Kubernetes, but I care more about the engineering habits around the tools: strong types, clear domain boundaries, solid CI harnesses, evals, observability, and deployment paths that don’t require heroics.

Originally from Berlin, I’ve lived in Chile and Kenya and now call Chicago home. When the laptop closes, you’ll find me mixing cocktails, cooking, exploring art, or chasing adventures with my two little daughters.
