The design of this site is based on the [Tailwind UI Spotlight](https://tailwindcss.com/plus/templates/spotlight) template and typeset in [Cartograph](https://connary.com/fonts/cartograph/) and uses [Lucide](https://lucide.dev/) icons.

The site is built with [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/), [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/), [MDX](https://mdxjs.com/) and [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/) and hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/).
